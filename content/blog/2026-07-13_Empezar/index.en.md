Starting something from scratch is always scary, or at least it is for me. That
feeling of looking at a blank page, or even worse, a new notebook, usually stops
me. I think I am always afraid of _starting badly_ because my mind believes
there is a _perfect start._ And searching for that perfect start is exactly what
stops me most of the time.

Writing down my thoughts helps me slow down, organize my mind, and make my ideas
real. For a long time, these ideas only stayed in my notebooks and personal
notes. Today, I think it is time to let some of them go a bit further. So, in
this space, I am going to write about the tools I use every day and the projects
I am working on.

To be honest, I do not know what this place will become. But I know I want to
have my notes together in one place. A place where I can return when I need to,
and hopefully, a place that can be useful to someone else too.

In the end, I think it is about being brave. And here I am again, in front of a
blank page. I feel a bit unsure, yes, but I know that if I want to do something,
the only way to start is by writing the first word.
